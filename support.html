Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log in to Guardian AI?

Use the email address and password provided by your supervisor or company administrator. If you have not received login credentials, contact your supervisor or email us at [email protected].

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your email address. You'll receive a reset link. If you don't see it, check your spam folder. Still having trouble? Email us and we'll help.

My field ticket didn't save. What do I do?

Make sure you have an active internet connection when saving. Guardian AI requires connectivity to save data to the cloud. If the problem continues, email us with details about your device and what happened.

Guardian Vision isn't analyzing my photo. What's wrong?

Check that you've granted the app permission to access your camera and photo library in your device settings. Also confirm you have an active internet connection. Photos are sent to our AI service for analysis and require connectivity.

How do I request Guardian AI for my company?
